====================Yoma is a solution offered by UNICEF Eastern and Southern Africa Regional Office (ESARO) and Generation Unlimited (GenU) to support the learning to earning journey of youth worldwide. ====================Everimpact is a SaaS solution that helps cities measure, identify, and monetize carbon emissions reductions and carbon sequestration. The organization operates in the thematic area of climate, specifically in the sub-categories of emissions, Fintech, and marketplace. Its solution aligns with SDG 9 (Industry, innovation, and infrastructure), SDG 11 (Sustainable cities and communities), and SDG 13 (Climate action). 

Through its digital carbon Monitoring Reporting Verification (MRV) solution, Everimpact enables cities to generate revenue from the voluntary carbon market, access green financing, and comply with reporting requirements. The solution employs satellite imagery, big data, and sensors to measure carbon emissions in near real-time and helps clients monitor emission reduction efforts. Everimpact also certifies projects to the highest standard and provides access to green financing.

The solution addresses the problem of cities lacking financial resources to invest in decarbonization efforts. By providing reliable carbon data and access to green finance, Everimpact allows cities, particularly in developing countries, to invest in decarbonization projects on a larger scale and at a faster pace. The solution also benefits deprived neighborhoods and cities in developed countries by facilitating the implementation of climate change projects.

Everimpact has already achieved measurable impact in Dijon, France, where it has measured 800kt of CO2 emissions per year and 20kt of CO2 emission reduction per year. It operates in multiple countries, including France, UK, Portugal, Germany, Norway, Japan, and Singapore, with plans to expand to India, Indonesia, Japan, Malaysia, and Canada. The organization has partnered with entities such as the European Commission, Asian Development Bank, CNES, Google, City Finance Lab, South Pole, BT, and EDF.

By providing cities with the means to measure and monetize their carbon emissions reductions, Everimpact helps bridge the digital divide in the context of climate action. The solution allows cities in both developed and developing countries to invest in and implement decarbonization projects effectively.
